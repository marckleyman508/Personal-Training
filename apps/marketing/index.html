Core capabilities

Built for dynamic performance, not static plans.

Readiness intelligence

Scores physical, neurological, and psychological readiness from daily inputs to decide what the body can truly handle.

Adaptive session builder

Selects modalities, exercises, and tempos on demand. Downgrades load the moment risk thresholds are crossed.

Recovery guardrails

Inserts mobility and regeneration, never overrides pain flags, and supports restraint when discipline becomes counterproductive.

Nutrition strategy

Leads with protein sufficiency, adjusts calories by training stress, and monitors hydration and stimulant intake.
